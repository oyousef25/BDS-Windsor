About BDS Windsor

BDSWindsor was established in 2023 by the local Windsor community in response to the genocidal assault on Gaza, joining the global bds movement in aid of Palestine and the Palestinian cause and becoming the Windsor Chapter of the Canadian BDS Coalition.

Our Mission

BDSWindsor supports the 2005 call from Palestinian civil society and all of its associated demands with the additional demand that Israel immediately stop the genocidal assault on Gaza and violence by both the Israeli military and settlers in the West Bank.

Frequently Asked Questions

Some common questions you may have about BDS Windsor, or BDS in general

What is BDS (short version)?

BDS stands for Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions. It is a non-violent movement that aims to pressure Israel to comply with international law and end its occupation of Palestinian.

How to find out if a certain product/brand supports Israel?

There are a lot of resources that can be used to find out if a product/brand supports the Israeli Occupation, Such as;

https://bdnaash.com/

https://bdsmovement.net/Act-Now-Against-These-Companies-Profiting-From-Genocide

https://bdscoalition.ca/boycott-list-of-shame/

https://bdsmovement.net/get-involved/what-to-boycott

(Specifically for cosmetics)

http://www.theblushinggiraffe.com/p/ethical-beauty.html

Do I have to quit my job if I work for a business that supports Israeli occupation?

There is no need to quit your job to answer the BDS call.

What are the main goals of BDS?

The BDS movement has three main goals: ending the Israeli occupation and colonization of Palestinian land, ensuring equal rights for Palestinian citizens of Israel, and promoting the right of return for the displaced Palestinian refugees.
